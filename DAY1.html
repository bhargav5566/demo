Mahesh Babu, born Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu on August 9, 1975, is a top-tier Indian actor and producer in Telugu cinema, nicknamed the "Prince of Tollywood" for his immense popularity, style, and box-office success in hits like Pokiri, Dookudu, and Maharshi. Son of veteran actor Krishna, he started as a child artist before his successful lead debut in Raja Kumarudu (1999), winning numerous awards, and is also known for his significant philanthropy, supporting children's health and adopting villages for development. He is married to actress Namrata Shirodkar, with whom he has two children, Gautham and Sitara.
